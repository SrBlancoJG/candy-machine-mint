The Rarity System
There is a rarity hierarchy in the project, that is represented by the colored frame and logo on each NFT.

The most beautiful and colorful of the collection have been manually selected to be the rarest and the rest have been programmatically sorted and classified based on their properties to represent their rarity.

The Pack System
Our rarity system is tied with our innovative pack system.

The 1000 NFTs that will be available for minting at our site are going to be divided into 10 packs. Each one of these packs will contain 100 NFTs:
  64 Commons

  1  UltraRare

The Fee System
If you are one of the addresses that mints a NebulaSol NFT on the Alpha Stage or you get a Promotional NFT you will be in the Pioneer Pool, and we'll divide the trade fees between all the directions in the pool.

So every time someone trades a NebulaSol NFT in any marketplace you will make a profit.
